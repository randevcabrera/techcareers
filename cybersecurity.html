What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity involves protecting computer systems and networks from information disclosure, theft, or damage to their hardware, software, or electronic data.


Career Options:


  • Security Analyst
  • Penetration Tester
  • Security Architect

    • Bachelor of Science in Information Systems (BSIS) - Major in Cybersecurity -
      Ideal for cybersecurity roles in business and IT governance.

    • Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering (BSCpE) -
