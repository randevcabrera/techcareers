What is a Database Manager?

Database management involves the administration and maintenance of database systems to ensure data is stored, organized, and accessed efficiently and securely. Database managers oversee database design, performance, backup, and recovery.


Your Career Options:


  • Database Administrator (DBA)
  • Data Analyst
  • Data Engineer

Recommended College Courses for Database Management!


  • Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) - Specialization in Database Management -
    Covers database design, SQL, cloud databases, and administration.

  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS) -
    Focuses on algorithms, data structures, and database systems.

  • Bachelor of Science in Information Systems (BSIS) -
    Combines business processes with database management and IT solutions.

  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering (BSCpE) -
    Covers both hardware and software aspects of database management.
